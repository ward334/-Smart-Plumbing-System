<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Neo-Brutalism / Technical Precision**
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Function over Form**: Prioritize raw data visibility and clear actions.
  2.  **High Contrast**: Use stark blacks and distinct blues against a pure white background.
  3.  **Modular Grid**: Strict alignment to grid lines, resembling engineering blueprints.
  4.  **Systematic Typography**: Monospaced fonts for data, bold sans-serif for headings.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Background**: Pure White (#FFFFFF) for clinical cleanliness.
  - **Primary**: Safety Blue (#0056D2) representing industrial water systems.
  - **Alert**: Signal Red (#D32F2F) for immediate attention.
  - **Text**: Jet Black (#1A1A1A) for maximum readability.
  - *Intent*: To convey reliability, precision, and an engineering-grade tool.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Dashboard-First**: Dense information display with clear borders.
  - **Sidebar Navigation**: Fixed left sidebar for quick access to tools.
  - **Card-Based Content**: Each sensor or alert lives in a clearly defined card with hard borders.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Visible Grid Lines**: Subtle gray lines separating sections.
  - **Technical Icons**: Stroke-based icons resembling CAD symbols.
  - **Monospace Data**: Timestamps and sensor IDs in a coding font (e.g., JetBrains Mono).

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Instant Feedback**: Buttons have sharp hover states (invert colors).
  - **No-Nonsense Transitions**: Fast, linear transitions (0.1s).

  **Animation**:
  - **Blinking Cursors**: For alerts or active states.
  - **Slide-in Panels**: Information panels slide in from the right with no bounce.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: `Chivo` or `Archivo` (Bold, Industrial).
  - **Body**: `Inter` (Clean, legible).
  - **Data**: `JetBrains Mono` or `Roboto Mono`.
</idea>

  - **Primary**: Ocean Blue (#0EA5E9) with gradients to lighter cyan.
  - **Alert**: Soft Coral Red (#F43F5E).
  - **Text**: Slate Gray (#334155) for a softer, modern look.
  - *Intent*: To make complex plumbing data feel accessible and modern (SaaS style).

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Central Focus**: Key content centered with ample margins.
  - **Floating Elements**: Buttons and cards float above the background.
  - **Asymmetric Balance**: Image on one side, text on the other for the landing page.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Glassmorphism**: Slight blur on overlay panels (alerts).
  - **Wave Motifs**: Subtle SVG wave dividers or background patterns.
  - **Pill-Shaped Buttons**: Fully rounded buttons.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Smooth Flow**: Hover effects that gently lift elements (transform: translateY).
  - **Ripple Effects**: Click interactions create a ripple.

  **Animation**:
  - **Fade & Float**: Elements fade in and float up gently.
  - **Pulse**: Sensor points pulse softly like a heartbeat.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: `Outfit` or `Poppins` (Geometric, Friendly).
  - **Body**: `DM Sans` or `Lato`.

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Swiss Style / International Typographic**
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Objective Clarity**: Information is presented without decoration.
  2.  **Asymmetric Layouts**: Dynamic positioning of text and images.
  3.  **Strong Typography**: Large, bold type as the primary visual element.
  4.  **Negative Space**: Heavy use of white space to guide the eye.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Background**: White (#FFFFFF).
  - **Primary**: Cobalt Blue (#0047AB) - deep, classic, professional.
  - **Alert**: Vermilion (#E34234).
  - **Text**: Dark Navy (#0A192F).
  - *Intent*: To project authority, professionalism, and timelessness.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Grid-Based but Asymmetric**: Elements align to a grid but don't fill every cell.
  - **Typographic Hierarchy**: Size and weight denote importance, not boxes.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Oversized Headings**: Titles are very large and bold.
  - **Thick Rule Lines**: Heavy horizontal lines to separate sections.
  - **Photographic Integration**: High-quality pipe images integrated with text wrapping.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Underline Reveals**: Links and buttons reveal thick underlines on hover.
  - **Color Shifts**: Text changes color on interaction.

  **Animation**:
  - **Typography Reveals**: Text slides up from a mask.
  - **Geometric Transitions**: Page transitions using sliding blocks of color.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: `Helvetica Now` or `Public Sans` (Bold, Tight tracking).
  - **Body**: `Public Sans` (Regular).
</idea>
</text>
